Dynamic Welfare Sanstha

Fighting for human rights, dignity, and justice through peaceful protest and activism.

Our Profile

Dynamic Welfare Sanstha is a Mumbai-based non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to upholding and protecting human rights while striving to improve society. Founded by Mr. Abdul Rauf Barudgar, the president of the organization, it was established to address various social challenges.

Office: 2-Vanmali Chal, Tejpal Road, near Nehru Road, Vile Parle East, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400056, India.

Our Vision

To create a society where all individuals, regardless of their background, have equal opportunities to thrive and lead fulfilling lives.

Our Mission

To empower local underserved communities through education, healthcare, skill development, and socio-economic initiatives, enabling them to become self-reliant and contribute to society.

We believe in the saying "Each one, teach one." Our utmost emphasis is on education, making it accessible to all.

Our Goals

  • Awareness and Education: Conducting workshops with schools to teach about hygiene, water conservation, and rights.
  • Legal Assistance: Free legal aid for human rights issues. Advocated against CAA-NRC in 2020.
  • Community Development: Regular beach clean-up drives and health campaigns to uplift communities.
  • Advocacy: Worked with policymakers to fight for affected communities and influence human rights laws.

Our Team

Dynamic Welfare Sanstha is powered by a dedicated team of volunteers and professionals who are passionate about making a difference. Our team members come from diverse backgrounds, bringing a wealth of experience and expertise to our initiatives.

We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork. Our volunteers are the backbone of our organization, working tirelessly to implement our programs and reach out to communities in need.

Raising Our Voice

Dynamic Welfare Sanstha stands at the forefront of advocacy and protest, ensuring voices of the marginalized are heard loud and clear. We organize peaceful protests and awareness campaigns to fight against injustices and promote equality.

Our team actively participates in various social movements, raising awareness about critical issues such as human rights violations, environmental concerns, and social justice. We believe in the power of collective action to bring about meaningful change.

Together, we can make a difference!

Education & Awareness

We are committed to leveraging the transformative power of education to improve lives and build a brighter future. Our initiatives are designed to provide access to quality education while fostering awareness about crucial social issues. Through workshops and seminars, we aim to empower students with knowledge about hygiene, water conservation, and their rights, ensuring they grow into informed and responsible citizens. Additionally, we organize community events to promote education and spread awareness, striving to make learning accessible to everyone. Together, we can create a world where education becomes a catalyst for positive change.

We believe that education is a powerful tool for social change. Our team is dedicated to providing educational resources and opportunities to underserved communities, ensuring that every individual has the chance to learn and grow.
